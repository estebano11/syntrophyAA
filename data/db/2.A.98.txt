<TR><TD COLSPAN=5><a name="description"></a>&nbsp;<p><p align='center'><strong>&nbsp; 2.A.98 The Putative Sulfate Exporter (PSE) Family </strong></p>
<p>Many putative 10 or 11 TMS bacterial proteins comprise the PSE family.  They are found in diverse bacteria and archaea.  The genes encoding some of these homologues may be induced by growth in the presence of cysteate (<em>suyZ</em>) or taurine (<em>tauZ</em>) (COG2855; <span id='ref3511.15758220' class='cite'>Rein <em>et al</em>., 2005</span>).
