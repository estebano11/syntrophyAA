<p>&nbsp; The DnaJ family members are found in several TC families: 1.P.1, 3.A.16, 3.A.30 and 3.A.5.&nbsp; These proteins are chaparone proteins that often function with DnaK (<span id="ref34554699.32305462" class="cite">Barriot <em>et al.</em> 2020</span>).
