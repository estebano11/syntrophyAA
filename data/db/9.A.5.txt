<TR><TD COLSPAN=5><a name="description"></a>&nbsp;<p><p style='text-align: center;'><strong>9.A.5 The Putative Arginine Transporter (ArgW) Family</strong></p>
<p style='text-align: left;'>The putative arginine transporter (ArgW) of <em>Shewanella oneidensis</em> contains 452aas with 10 putative TMSs in a 4+2+4 arrangement and N- and C- termini predicted inside. The <em>Geobacillus</em> homologue, HisW, contains 441aas with 10 or 11 putative TMSs; both N- and C- termini are inside. It is called the COG3314 family or GATE Superfamily (<span id='ref7007.18931129' class='cite'>Rodionov <em>et al.</em>, 2009</span>).</p>
<p style='text-align: left;'>The reaction believed to be catalyzed by ArgW is:</p>
