<TR><TD COLSPAN=5><a name="description"></a>&nbsp;<p><p style='text-align: center;'><strong>9.B.101&nbsp;The Cytotoxin-associated Gene Product (CagA) Family</strong></p>
<p>Sequence analyses of the virulence determinant CagA revealed three main groups strikingly similar to the three groups of VacA sequences (see TC family 1.C.9). Thus, positive selection has shaped the phylogenetic structure of VacA and CagA, and each of these virulence determinants has evolved separately from the core genome. CagA may also be related to members of the <em>Bacillus thuringiensis</em> Vegetative Insecticidal Protein-3 (Vip3) Family (e.g., 1.C.105.2.2).</p>
